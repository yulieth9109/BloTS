About Us

We Help Our Clients To Grow Their Business

BloTs is a startup initiative with a base in Chemnitz, Germany. Founded by a multicultural women's team with the mission to unlock the benefits of the blockchain outside the typical sectors of applications since blockchain is transforming traditional operations, eliminating middlemen, and enforcing safe and secure transactions.

We want to take care of client satisfaction through continued communication, protection of clients’ data and privacy, and provision of services on time.

Mission

To provide high-quality technology solutions that enhance the processes and interactions between our clients and their customers while stretching trust levels through blockchain technology.

Vision

Become a leading provider of blockchain solutions in the region, helping our clients to successfully apply technology to overcome their own challenges.

Purpose

To make life easy with intuitive and trustworthy tech solutions that adapt to the constant changes in the world.
